Cookies & Storage Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2026

This policy explains how MyWalkup uses local storage technologies on your device. As a native mobile application, we use secure on-device storage rather than traditional browser cookies.

What We Store on Your Device

Secure Keychain Storage

We use Apple's Keychain Services to securely store:

  • Authentication tokens - Keep you signed in between sessions
  • Session credentials - Securely authenticate API requests

Keychain data is encrypted by iOS and protected by your device passcode or biometrics.

Local File Storage

We cache certain files locally to improve performance:

  • Audio files - Walkup songs and AI-generated announcements are cached to reduce loading times and data usage
  • Images - Profile photos and team logos are cached for faster display

Cached files are stored in your device's temporary storage and may be automatically cleared by iOS when storage is low.

App Preferences

We store your preferences locally to remember your settings:

  • Audio playback preferences
  • Notification settings
  • Display preferences

Third-Party Storage

Some third-party services integrated into our app may store data locally:

Service Purpose Data Stored
Firebase Analytics & crash reporting Anonymous usage identifiers
RevenueCat Subscription management Purchase verification tokens

How Long Data Is Stored

  • Authentication tokens - Until you log out or they expire
  • Cached audio/images - Up to 7 days, or until cleared by iOS
  • Preferences - Until you uninstall the app or reset settings

Managing Stored Data

You can control data stored on your device:

  • Log out - Clears your authentication tokens
  • Delete account - Removes all your data from our servers (available in app settings)
  • Uninstall the app - Removes all locally stored data
  • Clear app cache - Go to iOS Settings → General → iPhone Storage → MyWalkup → Offload App

No Web Tracking Cookies

MyWalkup is a native iOS application and does not use traditional web browser cookies for tracking. We do not track you across other apps or websites.
